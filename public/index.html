Core Tech Stack

Autonomous Capabilities

Four interconnected operational pillars driving fail-safe execution and intelligence distribution at the tactical edge.

CAP_01

Perception

Dynamic sensor fusion translating physical environments into high-fidelity spatial telemetry at scale.

Multi-modal perception pipeline integrating high-density solid-state LiDAR, stereoscopic optical tracking, and radar arrays. Full spatial awareness matching localized mapping with global drift mitigation metrics.
CAP_02

Control

Adaptive actuation mapping high-frequency flight corrections in real time at the physical edge.

Fault-tolerant firmware executing distributed motor torque allocations, torque compensation vectors, and trajectory planning models at sub-millisecond rates directly on embedded hardware.
CAP_03

Orchestration

Distributed network protocol enabling cooperative mapping and collision avoidance.

Decentralized mesh networking architectures hosting local task allocations, dynamic fleet path planning, and decentralized obstacle grid evaluations, operating with or without global telemetry links.
CAP_04

Safety Envelope

Deterministic geoconstraints enforcing emergency procedures and fallback states.

Rigidly programmed watchdog routines containing airspace geofences, physical return-to-base trigger layers, structural failure mitigation profiles, and automated safe-descent sequences.
